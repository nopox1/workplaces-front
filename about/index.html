About Workplaces in Cyprus

Hi! I'm Vladimir, a digital nomad.

Many of my friends have asked me, "Where to work with a laptop in Cyprus?" The solution is now available on this website. The map shows suitable cafes and coworking spaces. You can filter them by socket availability, space size, cuisine, noise, busyness, view, and so on.

And don't forget to buy a coffee!

Rating: Vladimir's rate

I rate each place on a scale of 1 to 5. It's based on the busyness, cuisine, noise, size, sockets, and view of the space with varying weights.
